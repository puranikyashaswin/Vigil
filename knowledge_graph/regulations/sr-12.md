# Safety Regulation SR-12: Valve Operations

This regulation defines limits for pressure operations:
- Safety bypass valves (including V-202) must not exceed 100 PSI under any operating condition.
- Any setting above 100 PSI violates overpressure protocols and creates severe containment hazards.
