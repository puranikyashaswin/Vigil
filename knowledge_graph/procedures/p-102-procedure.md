# Process Procedure P-102: Pump safety and operations

Safety guidelines and regulations for Pump P-102:
- Pump P-102 handles highly hazardous process fluids and chemicals in the main production line.
- The operation, maintenance, and testing of pump P-102 must comply strictly with process safety management standards under 29 CFR 1910.119.
- This equipment is subject to risk management provisions under the Clean Air Act Amendments.
- All technician maintenance, pressure checks, and calibration procedures must be fully documented and audited every 6 months.
